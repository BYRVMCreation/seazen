SeaZen is a meticulously designed seafront apartment in Sitges, on the coast of Catalonia, Spain. With 120 m² of refined living space, floor-to-ceiling windows, and a private terrace overlooking the Mediterranean, it offers an unparalleled experience of calm.

Ideal for couples or small families — wake to the sound of waves, spend your days at the water's edge, and return each evening to a home that breathes simplicity.

Sitges

Beyond the apartment

Steps from your door — the best of the Costa Dorada's most beloved town.

Platja de la Fragata
Sitges' iconic beach — clear water, golden light, and the old town as backdrop. 4 minutes on foot.
Old Town & Promenade
Whitewashed lanes, a baroque church above the sea, and a seafront passeig lined with terrace restaurants.
Wine & Cava Country
The Penedès wine region begins 15 minutes inland. Cellar tours, tastings, and endless vineyards.
Museu Cau Ferrat
Santiago Rusiñol's clifftop house-museum — Picasso, El Greco, and Art Nouveau in a breathtaking setting.
Barcelona in 35 Minutes
Direct train puts you in the heart of Barcelona — Gaudí, Boqueria, Born — and back for sunset.
Seafood & Tapas
Fresh catch from the fishing boats. From humble chiringuitos to celebrated chefs — Sitges eats exceptionally well.
