Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2025

SeaZen ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our website or submit a booking request, we may collect the following information:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Booking dates, guest count, and special requests
  • Device and browser information (via cookies)
  • How you found us (referral source)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to:

  • Process and confirm your reservation enquiry
  • Communicate booking details and updates
  • Improve our website experience
  • Send occasional newsletters, only if you have opted in

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share it with trusted third-party services (e.g. booking platforms) strictly to fulfil your reservation.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to remember your preferences and analyse site traffic. You may disable cookies in your browser settings; however, some features may not function correctly.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined above, or as required by law. You may request deletion at any time.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection law you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@seazen.com.
