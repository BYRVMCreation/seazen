Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2025

We understand that plans can change. The following policy outlines the terms that apply when a confirmed reservation is cancelled by the guest.

1. Cancellation by Guest

All cancellation requests must be submitted in writing to hello@seazen.com. The cancellation date is the date on which SeaZen receives written notice.

Notice given before arrival Refund
More than 90 daysFull refund of deposit (less €150 admin fee)
60 – 90 days50% of total rental fee refunded
30 – 59 days25% of total rental fee refunded
Less than 30 daysNo refund

2. Cancellation by SeaZen

In the unlikely event that SeaZen must cancel a confirmed reservation (e.g. due to unforeseen circumstances such as structural damage or force majeure), guests will receive a full refund of all amounts paid. SeaZen will make every effort to suggest an alternative dates or property.

3. Force Majeure

SeaZen shall not be liable for cancellations caused by events beyond its reasonable control, including but not limited to natural disasters, governmental restrictions, or pandemic-related travel bans. In such cases, SeaZen will offer a credit or rescheduling wherever possible.

4. Travel Insurance

We strongly recommend that all guests purchase comprehensive travel insurance that covers cancellation, curtailment, and personal liability. SeaZen cannot be held responsible for costs incurred due to a cancelled stay.

5. Changes to Booking

Date changes are subject to availability and must be requested in writing. A €75 amendment fee applies. Changes within 30 days of arrival are treated as a cancellation and re-booking.

6. Contact

For cancellations or amendments, please contact us at hello@seazen.com or call +34 600 000 000.
